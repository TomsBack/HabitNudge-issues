# HabitNudge Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

HabitNudge does not collect, sell, share, transmit, or store personal data outside your browser.

HabitNudge runs locally in Chrome to manage small habit reminders, schedules, progress, snoozes, skips, and reminder display. Habit data and progress are processed on your device.

## Data Stored Locally

HabitNudge stores settings, habits, reminder schedules, progress, snooze/skip state, and short local history in Chrome extension storage. This data stays in your browser unless you use browser sync features controlled by Chrome.

## Page Interaction

HabitNudge uses a content script only to display an in-page reminder and send Done, Snooze, or Skip actions back to the extension. It does not read, collect, or transmit page content.

## Data Collection

HabitNudge does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, external tracking, remote logging, or external servers. It does not transmit habits, browsing history, page content, or settings to the developer or any third party.

## Permissions

HabitNudge requests browser permissions only to provide local reminder features: local storage for habits and progress, alarms for schedules, notifications for reminders, tab access for sending in-page reminder messages, and content-script access to display the reminder overlay.
